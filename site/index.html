Pensieve

Quickly capture ideas and remember them with the notes app you'll actually use.

Inspiration, great ideas, tasks, and to-dos pop into your head at inconvenient times and places.

Pensieve helps you capture those thoughts and keep your attention on what you're doing now. Jot them down with Pensieve and move on. When you check your email later, you'll find notes from Pensieve waiting for you.

The app is free to download and free to use. An optional All Access subscription is available for Pensieve power users.

Pensieve is the idea capture app for writers, creators, ADHD brains, multitaskers, and people who live in motion.

Pensieve is developed by Josh Beeler at Window Seat Labs in Portland, Oregon.
