Pensieve Features

Capture Ideas Instantly

Immediately start typing and press the Send button. Pensieve handles the rest and you get an email in your inbox.

Record Audio, Get Transcripts

Save yourself the trouble of typing and say what's on your mind. Pensieve will email you the audio and a text transcript. Great for driving and thinking walks.

Shutter Button = Send Button

Pictures are worth a thousand words. The Pensieve camera takes photos and emails them to you in one tap. Or, you can attach the photos to your message. Great for research when shopping or traveling.

Easily Attach Files

Attach your most recent photos, or pick any photo or file on your phone.

Share Almost Anything

Pensieve Share helps you get links, images, text, and more from other apps into your inbox.

Works Offline

Use Pensieve anywhere, anytime, even without an internet connection. Your drafts are saved on your phone to be sent when you go back online.
